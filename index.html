All about sleep




what is sleep?

Sleep is a state of reduced mental and physical activity in which consciousness is altered and sensory activity is inhibited to a certain extent. During sleep, there is a decrease in muscle activity, and interactions with the surrounding environment.

why is sleep inportant?

Good sleep improves your brain performance, mood, and health.

what is the main benefit of sleep?

Sleep helps you think more clearly, have quicker reflexes and focus better.

what happens if you dont sleep?

The signals your body sends may also be delayed, decreasing your coordination and increasing your risk for accidents.
